About the Helpdesk

The MIET Smart Campus Helpdesk is a digital platform that allows students to report issues related to hostels, labs, classrooms, library, transport, and more. Our team ensures timely resolution and keeps you updated on the status of your requests.

"Our mission is to provide a seamless experience for every student, ensuring their concerns are heard and resolved promptly."

How It Works

  1. Fill the complaint form below with your details and issue
  2. Our team reviews and assigns the complaint
  3. Track progress through email notifications
  4. Confirm resolution once satisfied

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my complaint status?

After submitting a complaint, you will receive email updates. You can also check the status by logging into the student portal.

How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

Most complaints are resolved within 48 hours. Urgent issues are prioritised and resolved within 24 hours.

Can I submit an anonymous complaint?

Yes, you may leave the name field as "Anonymous". However, providing contact details helps us resolve issues faster.
